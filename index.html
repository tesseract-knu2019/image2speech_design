Tesseract for Kor is a Tesseract OCR trained kor + eng together. you can check the trained result here! trained data (kor+eng) .

The original Tesseract was an OCR that could only interpret each language. My team didn't stay here, improved the existing Tesseract. it can provide better service by learning Korean and English together and a service to replace text files with voice.

Check this service through documents commonly found around articles, papers, etc.

"Use this if you want a text file with a mixture of Korean and English.."
